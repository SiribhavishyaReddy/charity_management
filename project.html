about us

"No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted."

At Little Helpers Charity and Fundraising, we aim to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need. Our mission is to provide support, resources, and hope to individuals and communities facing challenges, whether through poverty, illness, or other hardships. We believe that every small act of kindness can create a ripple effect of positive change. Our mission is to empower and uplift those in our community by providing essential services, educational opportunities, and emotional support. We strive to create a world where everyone has access to the resources they need to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.
